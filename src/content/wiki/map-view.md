Every memo with a location appears as a pin on the map. The map view lets you wander your past by place rather than by time.

## Smart Clustering

Nearby memos automatically group into blue cluster markers with counts. Zoom in to see individual pins, or zoom out to get the big picture at a glance.

## Photo Pins & Cluster Carousel

If a memo has a photo, the pin shows a thumbnail. Tap a cluster to flip through the memos it contains in a small carousel — useful when several memos pile up in one spot.

## Bottom Detail Card

Tap any pin to pull up a detail card from the bottom of the screen. Drag it up if you want to read more, or tap through to the full memo.
