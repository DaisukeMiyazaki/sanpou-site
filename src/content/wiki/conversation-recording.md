Some memos aren't a single thought — they're a conversation. Conversation mode records longer dialogue, transcribes it, and keeps the original audio so you can re-listen later. Useful for interviews, walking chats with a friend, or capturing a story you don't want to summarize on the fly.

This feature is still in **beta**: transcription accuracy depends on background noise and how clearly each speaker talks, and longer recordings consume more device storage.
