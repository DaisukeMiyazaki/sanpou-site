---
title: "Step Tracking"
description: "Each memo records your step count from HealthKit, so you can see how far you'd walked before a thought arrived."
firstAddedVersion: "1.0.0"
lastUpdated: "2026-05-14"
---

Walks and ideas are entangled. Sanpou queries HealthKit at the moment you save a memo and records the steps you'd accumulated that day. The number lives in the memo metadata and travels with it on export.

Over time, this gives you a quiet trail of context: which thoughts came after a long walk, which after a short one. Sanpou doesn't push the number in your face — it's there when you want to look.
