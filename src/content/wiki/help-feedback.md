## In-App Help

The memo and map screens each have a dedicated help page that walks you through the gestures, filters, and less-obvious touches. There are also small tip overlays that surface contextually as you use features for the first time.

## Feedback Form

If something feels off or you have an idea, the in-app feedback form sends a note directly to the developer. No issue tracker login, no email thread — just a text box.

Sanpou is built by one person, and the feedback form is the most direct line to that person.
