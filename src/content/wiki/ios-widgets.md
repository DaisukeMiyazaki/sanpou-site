Sanpou wants to be one tap away. The app ships with several widgets so you can capture a memo before the thought is gone.

## Quick Voice Recording

A home-screen widget that starts a voice memo instantly. Available per language — there's a dedicated Japanese widget and a Dutch one, so you don't have to switch settings before recording.

## Lock Screen Text Entry

Type a memo from the lock screen without unlocking the device. Especially useful for quiet places.

## Lock Screen Voice & Photo

Lock screen widgets for one-tap voice recording and a photo-first memo (open the camera straight from the lock screen, snap, save).

## Language-Specific Lock Screen Recording

If you record primarily in Dutch (or any of the supported languages), there's a lock screen widget that records directly in that language — no language picker needed.
