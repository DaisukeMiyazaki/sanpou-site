The map gets more useful as your memo history grows. Filters help you focus.

## Keyword Search

Search across all memo text — the matching pins stay visible, the rest fade out. Good for finding "that thought about coffee" or every memo that mentions a friend's name.

## Time Range

Limit the map to memos from a particular time window. Last week, last month, last summer. The view updates as you adjust.

## Weather Category

Show only sunny-day memos, only rainy ones, or only the foggy mornings. Pairs well with [Auto Weather Tagging](/sanpou-site/wiki/weather-tagging/).
