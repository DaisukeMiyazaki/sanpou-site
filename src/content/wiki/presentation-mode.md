Sometimes a memo isn't just for you. Presentation Mode mirrors curated memo snapshots to an external display (TV, projector, AirPlay), and your phone becomes the remote.

This feature is still in **beta**. It lives under **Beta features** in Settings as the **Presentations** toggle — it's on by default, and turning it off hides the share button on the Map tab and the Presentations tab.

Useful for:

- **Talks** — flip through location-anchored memos as visual aids during a presentation
- **Exhibitions** — set up an ambient slideshow of memos with photos and ambient audio
- **Sharing** — show family or friends a walk you took, on a screen larger than your phone

Snapshots are saved separately from your memo list, so you can curate a specific set for a specific occasion without touching the originals.
