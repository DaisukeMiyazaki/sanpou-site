A memo is more than words. Ambient Audio records a short clip of the soundscape around you at the moment of saving — the wind, distant traffic, a café's background hum — and stores it alongside the memo.

Months later, replaying that ambient clip is often what brings the memory back, more vividly than the text alone. Think of it as a sonic photograph.
