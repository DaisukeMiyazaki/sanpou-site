## Conversation Recording (Beta)

Some of the best memos come out of conversations on a walk — with a friend, with yourself thinking out loud. Turn on Conversation mode and Sanpou will record and transcribe the whole thing, then attach it to your memo automatically.

## A New Memo Detail View

Tap into any memo to open a focused view with a sticky audio player that stays put as you scroll. Easier to read along while listening, or to skim the transcript without losing the play button.

## Polish and Stability

Small improvements throughout the app to make everyday capture feel smoother.
