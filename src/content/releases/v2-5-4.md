## Read Only What Matters

Text recognition used to scan the whole photo, picking up signs, labels, and background text you never meant to capture. Now you can crop first: drag the corners over the part you want, run recognition on just that area, and decide whether to keep the cropped image or the original. Skipping the crop and scanning the full photo still works with one tap.
