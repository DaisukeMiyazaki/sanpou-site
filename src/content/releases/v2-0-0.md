## Weather at a Glance

Ever tried to recall a memo but couldn't quite place it? Weather is one of those subtle cues that brings a moment back — "that idea I had on the rainy walk home." Starting with this update, every new memo automatically captures the current weather. You'll see a weather icon in the memo list, and you can filter by weather on the map. Note: weather data is recorded for new memos going forward; existing memos won't have weather info.

## Dutch Language Support

Sanpou now supports Dutch. Switch to Dutch in settings to record voice memos, and there's a dedicated lock screen widget for quick Dutch recordings too.

## Bug Fix

Fixed a bug where the circular lock screen widget wouldn't start transcribing.
