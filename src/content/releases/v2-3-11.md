## Multiple Photos & Videos per Memo

_Added in 2.3.5_

You can now attach several photos or short videos to a single memo, with pinch-to-zoom and the camera presets you'd expect (0.5x / 1x / 2x).

## Ambient Audio

_Added in 2.2.0_

Sanpou records a short clip of the soundscape around you when you save a memo — wind, café murmur, distant traffic — so you can audibly relive the moment later. A sonic photograph.

## Lock Screen Photo & Video

_Added in 2.3.4_

Start a memo with a photo or video straight from your lock screen. New widget, one tap, no unlock needed.

## Refreshed Home Screen

_Added in 2.3.11_

Smoother navigation throughout the app — small touches, but they add up.

## Presentation Mode

_Added in 2.3.8_

Show your memos on a bigger screen via AirPlay or HDMI. Curate a slideshow of moments with photos and ambient audio for talks, exhibitions, or sharing memories on a TV.
