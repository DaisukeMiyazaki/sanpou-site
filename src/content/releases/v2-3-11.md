## A Bit More Feedback While Saving

Saving a memo doesn't always finish instantly — Sanpou fetches a few things in the background (location, weather, the genius loci note) before everything settles in. Until now, that small wait was invisible, which sometimes left it unclear whether the save was truly done.

Starting with this update, a quiet loading spinner appears while metadata is still being fetched. You can keep using the app freely — the spinner just lets you know there's still a tiny bit of work happening behind the scenes.
