## Type Your Memos

Not every moment calls for speaking out loud — like when you're in a quiet café or on a crowded train. You can now type memos directly from the memo list — tap the text input button and the keyboard pops up instantly. There's also a new lock screen widget for quick text entry without even opening the app.
