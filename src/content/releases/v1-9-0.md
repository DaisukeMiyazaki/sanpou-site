## Manual Text Memo Input

You can now capture your thoughts even in environments where voice recording isn't ideal. We've added a new "Type a Memo" button directly in the memo list for quick manual entry.

- **Smart Focus:** The text editor now automatically triggers the keyboard upon entry, enabling a faster "thought-to-text" experience.
- **Dynamic UI:** The app intelligently switches its interface between "Voice Memo" and "Memo" modes based on your chosen input method.

## Complete Lock Screen Widget Overhaul

We've redesigned our Lock Screen widgets to give you one-tap access to exactly what you need.

- **New "Type Memo" Widget:** Start typing a memo instantly from your Lock Screen.
- **Language-Specific Recording:** Dedicated widgets for "Record (Japanese)" and "Record (English)" let you start voice capture without any additional steps.
- **Circular & Rectangular Support:** All widgets now support both compact Circular and informative Rectangular layouts.
- **Unified Visuals:** Recording icons now use a consistent style (`mic.fill`) for a cleaner look when multiple widgets are placed together.

## System Integration & Shortcuts

- **AppIntents & Siri:** You can now say "Type a memo with Sanpou" to Siri or use the new action in the Shortcuts app.
- **Deep Link Support:** Advanced users can now use `sanpou://memo?action=startTextMemo` for custom automation.
