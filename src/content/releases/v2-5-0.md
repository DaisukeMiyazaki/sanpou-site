## Photo Transcription

Got a photo of a sign, a menu, or a handwritten note? Tap the **Read text** button inside the photo's fullscreen preview (while writing a memo) or on the media carousel (for saved memos) to pull the text straight into your memo. The button no longer opens the camera — it works on the photo you're already looking at.

## Dutch Transcription

Dutch joins Japanese and English as a recognized language for photo transcription.

## Smarter Language Picker

The transcription language picker now follows your app language settings. If you only use one language it stays out of the way; otherwise it shows your primary with an optional secondary. The horizontal / vertical direction picker only appears when you're scanning Japanese.

## Fixes

- Editing a memo and removing the last attached photo now saves correctly — the photo no longer reappears.
- Fixed a flash of black when opening the transcription sheet on iOS 18 and later.
- Fixed a rare crash when inserting transcribed text into the editor.
