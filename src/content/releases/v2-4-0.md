## Silent Shutter

The in-app camera is now silent. No shutter click, no jarring sound when you want to capture something in a library, a café, or anywhere a click would feel intrusive.

Behind the scenes, Sanpou now captures stills through the video data output pipeline rather than the standard photo API — same image, no audible feedback. You'll notice the difference the next time you take a photo: it's just quiet.
