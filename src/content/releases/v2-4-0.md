## Silent Shutter

Take memo photos without the shutter sound. Sleeping kids, napping pets, and quiet cafés won't be disturbed.

Under the hood, Sanpou now captures stills through the video data output pipeline rather than the standard photo API — same image quality, no audible feedback.
