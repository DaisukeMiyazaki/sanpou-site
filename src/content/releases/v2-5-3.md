## Share the Whole Moment

Sharing a memo used to send only its words. Now the photos and videos you captured can travel with them. Tap share, and if a memo has media, you'll pick which pieces to include — everything's selected by default — before it heads off to Messages, your notes app, or a friend. The memo, the picture, the short clip of the wind: sent as one.
